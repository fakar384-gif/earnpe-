Frequently Asked Questions
1. How to complete a task?
To complete a task, click on the offer, read the instructions carefully, and click on the specific link provided. Once you complete the process (e.g., account opening), submit your proof.
2. When will I get my reward?
Once you submit the proof (screenshot/details), our team will verify it. Verification usually takes 24 to 48 hours. Upon successful verification, the reward will be credited to your wallet.
3. How to withdraw my money?
Go to the Wallet section, enter your UPI ID or Bank details, and click on Withdraw. The minimum withdrawal amount is ₹100.
4. Why was my lead rejected?
A lead can be rejected if the proof is invalid, if you already had an account with that specific app/bank, or if you did not use the link provided inside the EarnPe app.
