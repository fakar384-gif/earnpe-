Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to EarnPe. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the best experience and process your rewards, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Data: Name, Email address, Phone number, and Date of Birth.
  • Financial Data: UPI ID or Bank Account details (strictly for processing your withdrawal requests).
  • Usage Data: Device information, IP address, and how you interact with our app (e.g., offers clicked, tasks completed).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To track your completed tasks and credit rewards accurately.
  • To process your withdrawal requests securely.
  • To prevent fraud, fake referrals, and unauthorized activities.
  • To send you important notifications regarding app updates and offers.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following scenarios:

  • Service Providers: With third-party partners who help us securely process payments.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government regulations.
  • Affiliate Partners: Basic tracking data (excluding sensitive personal details) is shared with partner brands to verify successful lead generation.

4. Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Your payment details and passwords are encrypted and securely stored in our databases.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. If you wish to delete your EarnPe account and erase your data, please contact our support team.
